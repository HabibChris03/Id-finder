PRECAUTIONS

  • Consistent Storage: Always keep your ID card in the same place, such as a specific pocket in your wallet or bag.
  • Use a Holder or Lanyard: Use an ID holder or lanyard to keep it visible and secure, especially if you need to show it frequently.
  • Minimal Movement: Avoid taking your ID card out unnecessarily; only remove it when required.
  • Check Regularly: Develop a habit of checking for your ID card before leaving any location.
  • Digital Backup: Keep a digital copy or note of your ID number in a secure app for reference.
  • Routine Organization: Regularly organize your wallet or bag to ensure everything is in its place.
  • Label Your Items: If possible, label your ID card with your contact information in case it's lost and found.
